About Yatrik Distributors

Three decades of trusted financial excellence

Founded in 1995, Yatrik Distributors Private Limited is a registered Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) based in Kolkata. Over the past three decades, we have grown from a commodity distribution firm into a diversified financial services enterprise with a strong focus on structured financing, auto finance, B2B credit models, and strategic capital deployment.

We operate under full compliance with the Reserve Bank of India and continue to scale across financial verticals and regions with a healthy balance sheet and strong credibility.

Mission

To provide efficient, reliable, and responsible financial solutions to businesses and individuals—offering access to global wealth, empowering sustainable growth, and building long-term trust across Eastern India and beyond.

Vision

To be a premier NBFC-led financial powerhouse with deep local roots and global relationships—enabling wealth creation, strategic partnerships, and inclusive development through innovative financial services.

Core Strengths

  • Licensed NBFC with compliant and capital-efficient operations
  • 30+ years of consistent operational growth
  • Proven finance desk in B2B & B2C financial services
  • Strong regional influence and deep market expertise in East India
  • Trusted network of HNIs and institutional partners
  • Agile, relationship-driven, and innovation-ready approach

Regional Presence & Reach

Yatrik Distributors has a strong presence across West Bengal, Assam, Odisha, Bihar, and Chhattisgarh. We specialize in relationship-driven lending, asset-backed financing, and customized structuring—ensuring agility and responsiveness in a dynamic financial landscape.
